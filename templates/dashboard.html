Emission formula

How emissions are calculated
tim_co2    = distance × passengers × emission_factor
           (short 0.255 · medium 0.195 · long 0.147 kg CO₂/km/pax)

luggage_co2 = (distance / 1000) × luggage_kg × 0.5
total_co2   = (tim_co2 + luggage_co2) × cabin × (1 − SAF)
points_lost = total_co2 / 80
