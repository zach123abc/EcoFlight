How it works

Every school starts with 1,000 points. Log flights to deduct points based on real emissions. Earn them back through green actions. Ranked fairly by points per student.

01
Log Your Flights
Enter distance, cabin class, luggage weight and SAF percentage. Points are deducted proportional to real-world CO2 emissions.
02
Take Green Actions
Plant trees, collect waste, or produce solar energy to earn points back. Sustainable actions have real, measurable impact on your score.
03
Compete on the Leaderboard
Schools are ranked by points per student, so every pupil counts. See how your school stacks up against others nationwide.

Sustainable actions that earn points back

T
Collect Waste
Each kilogram of rubbish collected earns +0.5 points. A measurable, team-based activity for the whole school community.
S
Generate Solar Energy
Solar production earns points on a square-root scale. The more you generate, the more your efficiency compounds over time.
P
Plant Trees
Every tree planted earns +5 points. Trees absorb CO2 over decades — plant a forest and watch your score grow with it.
